For New Applicants

Frequently Asked Questions

Most declined applications are avoidable. Use this guide before you apply to improve your chances and present your best work.

What makes an application stand out?

Strong applications are specific. Mention your role experience, include portfolio or clip links, and explain how often you can contribute each week.

How long does review usually take?

Most reviews are completed within 3 to 7 days depending on queue size. Complex roles may include an extra evaluation step by the relevant team lead.

Can I apply for more than one role?

Yes, but apply for roles where you can consistently deliver quality work. Focused, realistic applications perform better than broad but vague ones.

What are the core behavior standards?

Respect communication channels, meet deadlines you commit to, respond to staff feedback, and represent the organization professionally across all platforms.

Application Checklist

Before pressing submit, verify each point below.

  1. Your Discord tag is correct and accepts friend requests.
  2. Your portfolio, clips, or sample links are public and accessible.
  3. You explained your availability with realistic weekly time commitment.
  4. You selected the role that best matches your strongest skill.
  5. You reviewed spelling and clarity to ensure staff can evaluate quickly.
